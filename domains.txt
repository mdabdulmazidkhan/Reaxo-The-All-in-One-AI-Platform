REGISTRATION TIPS
=================
- Check availability on: namecheap.com, godaddy.com, domains.google.com
- Consider .io for tech startups (most popular choice)
- Consider .ai specifically for AI platforms (trending)
- .app is also gaining popularity
- Avoid .co unless you want longer typing
- .dev is good for developer tools
- Shorter names are always better for memorability
- Avoid numbers and hyphens if possible

BRANDING CONSIDERATIONS
=======================
- Single syllable names are easiest to remember and pronounce
- Make sure it works internationally (no offensive meanings)
- Check social media availability (@yourname)
- Consider how it sounds when spoken aloud
- Ensure it's easy to spell
- Avoid names too similar to existing brands
